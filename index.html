FAQs

What do you need from me?

A customer list export from whatever you already use (PestPac, Jobber, ServiceTitan, or spreadsheets, all fine) and about 20 minutes. We handle everything else. Your data stays yours.

Yes, done right. We only message customers who gave your business their number and agreed to be contacted, every text identifies your business, and STOP is honored instantly and automatically. Compliance is built into the audit before a single message goes out.

No. Messages read like you texted them yourself: short, personal, from the owner. Your customers reply like it's you, because as far as they can tell, it is.
