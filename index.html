Rebooked Co

Customer messaging for local home service businesses

Rebooked Co helps independent pest control, pool service, HVAC, and lawn care companies stay connected with their own customers by text message — answering missed calls, confirming appointments, and reminding past customers when routine service is due.

What we do

We operate text messaging on behalf of the home service businesses you already know and trust. If you received a message through Rebooked Co, it came from a business you are a customer of, or a business you just called. We never message people who have no relationship with the business, and we never buy or sell contact lists.

Your choices

You can stop receiving messages from any business at any time by replying STOP, or get assistance by replying HELP. See our SMS Terms & Conditions and Privacy Policy for details.
